About

The art of seeing the unseen

Captured by Jlan is a photography practice built on the belief that the most powerful images live in the spaces between — between movement and stillness, light and dark, intention and accident.

Based in Montenegro, working worldwide. Every project is approached as a collaboration, with the goal of creating images that outlast the moment.
